## 1. Knowledge Episodes (the plaster)

### EP-FP-01 — Match IGNIVER density with gypsum+vermiculite (formulas 001–008)

- **Decision:** pursue low-gypsum/high-V2 (F002, F005). **Outcome:** partial.
